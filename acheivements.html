Achievements

Best Paper Award

The project won the best paper award in the 10th Global , Engineering, Science and Technology Conference.






A2i innovation fund

The project was supported by the Ministry of Information and Communication Technology of Bangladesh which then granted the project an A2i innovation fund of 10 lakh BDT for further development of the project.






Panasonic Idea Changes Life

The idea and the whole project was forcasted in Panasonic Idea Changes Life event and then the projected was also featured on the national television where the project first came into limelight.






Imagine Cup Bangladesh 2012

Imagine Cup is Microsoft's premiere student technology competition. Our concept of image processing based desease detection was materialized in this competition by Rahat Yasir and his team of developers and in this competition the team won the runners up prize.
